Webstock '13: How Designers Destroyed the World

Mike Monteiro

VIDEO

Throughout the video, Mike Monteiro talks about the importance and responsiblity that comes with being a designer. He believes that designers should be passionate, have a vision, have an end goal, be proud and care for what they create. He also mentions that being a designer isn't easy. People will be better than you, and you will be better then other, and that all designers will make mistaks. Overall, Monteiro is basically saying it's not just the content that is important for the designing aspect, imagination is impoortant as well. He wants designers to recognize the power the have and to use it successfully and thoughtfully.

  • Design is your job, not your clients. Do not put the work on them.
  • When something is badly designed and potentially harmful, you have one of three possible situations: ignore it, run it up the chain, the chain of command ignored.
  • Bad design makes it out into the World not through malicious intent but through no intent at all.

Mobile First

Luke Wroblewski

VIDEO

Luke Wroblewski talks about the current trends in mobile devices. He provides data that shows more and more people are surfing the web through mobile devices. Based on the increasing mobile internet usage, Wroblewski believes that web designers should design with the idea of "mobile first" in mind. He then talks about constraints, meaning, designers have to redesign based on screen sizes and formatting. Therefore, one has to know their audeince and figure out what content is important.

  • Growth=Opportunity
  • Constraints=Focus
  • Capabilities=Innovation
